What We Do

We are a non-profit 501(c)3 organization that was founded by Terry Figueroa, with its primary mission being to rescue Arabian horses in need. In addition to Arabians, ARM has helped many other breeds as well. Our goal is to rescue, rehabilitate and find loving, permanent homes for them.

Our secondary mission is educating people in the responsibility, commitment and knowledge which can provide them with the tools necessary to offer nurturing, healthy environments for their horses. We are of the belief that every horse deserves a caring, responsible, educated owner; and every person deserves the chance at owning one of God's most dignified and gracious creatures.

Short-Term Mission

Our project for 2022-2025 is to replace the rest of the interior fencing and to go green. We'd like to make the farm sustainable by getting a well and automatic waterers installed and putting up solar panels. This is another huge project but I have faith we can do it! This will be a 3 year plan for completion, so stay tuned. In the meanwhile enjoy the new fencing we got last year, 'cause we are.
