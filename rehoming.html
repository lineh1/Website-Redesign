ARM Re-Homing Horses


Important

The horses listed below are looking for a new job and a "forever" home. For various reasons their owners cannot keep them and have asked us to help place them in the right situations.Since we are full, at the moment, with true rescues, we are acting as the go-between for these horses, owners and their prospective new adopters. They are not in the custody of the Arabian Rescue and we can only provide the information as relayed to us. Re-Homing fees run $200.00, unless otherwise stated. A completed adoption application must be submitted, along with a $25.00 non-refundable, application fee (deposit not necessary). Once approved owner information will be provided. Re-Homing fee is due upon completion of the placement agreement, and the horse is ready to go home and if registered, you get the papers. Simple!


Shiloh(KY)

Meet Shiloh-this mare is well broke to ride, sound, no vices, just jigs, dances and spooks some. A bit much for her owner to ride. She is a 13yr old quarab, between 14-14.3hds and sweet. Owner is moving and really wants her to find a new home with someone who can appreciate her. She is located near Elizabethtown, KY until October, when we will have room to take her. Fee $600.00


Melody(KY)

This 8yr old girl needs a new address through no fault of any one. Situations change and she needs a home who will train her and give her a job. She loves grooming and attention and is a treat hound. She's around 14.2hds but unstarted. Choose a discipline and enjoy her next year. Located in KY, not far from Cincinnati OH. Fee $600.
