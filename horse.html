Recent News Short-Term Mission

Our last 2 boys are home and we are getting ready for winter. Costumes for the kids and hay in the barn. If you dress up with your horse or decorate your barn, enter our Halloween Costume Contest. 3 categories to choose from, horse, horse & rider or barn. It's $5.00 per entry and there will be prizes. Just email the pics to info@arabianrescuemission.org, Paypal $5 to donate@arabianrescuemission.org and then check out our event so you can share the link for your pictures. The ones with the most LIKES wins.

Our project for 2022-2025 is to replace the rest of the interior fencing and to go green. We'd like to make the farm sustainable by getting a well and automatic waterers installed and putting up solar panels. This is another huge project but I have faith we can do it! This will be a 3 year plan for completion, so stay tuned. In the meanwhile enjoy the new fencing we got last year, 'cause we are.
