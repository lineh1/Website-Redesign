HALOS FOR HORSES

Every horse is an angel, or at least, started that way. The forgotten, abandoned creatures on these pages had that taken away from them. We try to give them back their dignity, respect, wings and even HALOs.

You can be a HALO for a horse. For as little as $1.00 a month can make a difference in the life of just one horse. For less than the cost of a cup of coffee you can help feed, clothe and care for our horses.

So help save a life and give back! Click on the Subscribe link below to have your monthly HALO donation automatically sent, safely and securely through PayPal. Remember even $5.00 buys a bale of hay each month!
