HOW ARM CAN HELP YOU-EDRF PROGRAMS


Arabian Rescue Mission, Inc. (ARM) is a 501(c)3 non-profit, voted top-rated for the last 10 consecutive years. We are located in South Central KY.

In line with our mission to help horses in need, focusing on Arabian horses, we have proposed a pro-active approach to helping them.

Since the beginning we have always been available to help re-home horses whose owners are unable to care for them due to life-changing situations. Whether it be posting, advertising and marketing them to future homes, accepting owner surrenders at our farms or traveling to care for and place entire herds from their locations, our approach has always been professional, empathetic and non-judgmental, keeping the horsesâ€™ best interest at the forefront.

Owner-Assistance

Our owner assistance program is slated to start in 2017. We have some seed money and are raising more. Applications will be accepted when we are ready to launch and this program will help keep horses in their homes while owners suffering from temporary setbacks, like divorce, death of a wage earner, job loss or medical emergencies, get back on their feet and/or while we re-home some or all of their horses.

Euthanasia Assistance

At times horses are too old or disabled to be able to be re-homed, continue living a quality life and need help crossing the Rainbow Bridge. At these times, we can offer financial assistance to those in need. Open to both private owners and rescues we will offer 50% (up to $100) euthanasia cost as funding is available

Applications are being accepted as of 1/1/17 for this financial aid.
